ABOUT ME

JONATHAN DEMPSEY

Hi! My name is Jonathan, and I’m a 23 year old student in my final year of Web Engineering / Multimedia Systems in IADT Dun Laoghaire. In November, I’ll be graduating from IADT with my BSc (Honours) in Multimedia Systems/Web Engineering with a Second Class Honours Grade 1 (2:1), exciting stuff!



IADT has taught me to be multi-disciplinary from working on a wide variety of projects, including coding in front-end and back-end for web applications, UI and UX design, using numerous adobe products (Photoshop, After Effects, etc.), research and working on team projects.



When I’m not spending time working on projects I can be found roaming the streets of Dublin on my trusty bicycle, sitting in front of my piano learning new songs, playing some video games on my Xbox or taking care of my three awesome cats.
