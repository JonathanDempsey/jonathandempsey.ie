The objective of this project was to create a news website with a home page and article page. The design doesnâ€™t use any images, videos or colour which meant design techniques were established to show contrast between the sites different sections.

Surrounding myself with physical newspapers was the first step in the design process. Irish and international newspapers were found and used as references. An article was taken from a German newspaper and a wireframe was created in Photoshop before coding the article into a HTML page. This was to familiarise myself with the process of designing in Photoshop and transferring that design into a web page using Bootstrap.

After finishing the German article, research was carried out to determine design concepts that would help create contrast within the website. The research included finding fonts that had a variety of weights and styles and ensuring they would be readable at small and large font sizes, using white space to consolidate segments of the web page, making use of the grey scale and establishing a page hierarchy. Multiple existing online news sites were also found to draw inspiration from.

Once the research was complete, a wireframe of the sites home and article page was created after multiple iterations were worked through. The wireframe design was then designed in the HTML pages with CSS and Bootstrap.
